CONTACT US

There are several ways to contact the Club. Please take a look at the links and contacts below to see which is most appropriate to help with your query.

General Club Enquiries Tottenham Hotspur Football Club Bill Nicholson Way 748 High Road Tottenham London N17 0AP

Telephone: 0344 499 5000


Tottenham Hotspur is proud of its relationship with fans and constantly strives to improve supporters' match day experience and day-to-day interaction with the Club.

If you do have reason to complain on match day, please speak to a steward or member of our staff. We find that 90% of all complaints can be resolved at the time of the initial problem. If your complaint cannot be resolved on the day or it is about another matter, please select the appropriate contact us and we will endeavour to reply to you as quickly as possible and certainly within five working days.
Please do not direct e-mail queries to several different addresses as this slows the Club's response times and may mean that your query is overlooked.
